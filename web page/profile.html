About Me

Designing with intention,
thinking in systems.

Hey, I'm Arjun — an entry-level UI/UX designer passionate about building digital products that genuinely work for people. I got into design because I kept noticing how frustrating bad interfaces can be, and I wanted to fix that.

My approach starts with understanding the user — their goals, frustrations, and mental models — before I ever open Figma. I believe the best designs are invisible: when everything just flows, users don't think about the interface, they think about their goal.

Currently deepening my skills in interaction design, design systems, and the fascinating overlap between UX and product strategy.

HealthTrack — Wellness App Redesign

Redesigning a health monitoring app to cut cognitive overload, rebuild daily habit loops, and make personal wellness feel approachable rather than clinical.

Problem Statement
68% of users abandoned the app within 7 days due to a cluttered, overwhelming dashboard that made tracking feel like homework.
My Role
End-to-end UI/UX — user research, journey mapping, wireframing, high-fidelity UI, and interactive Figma prototype.

✅ Outcome

StudyFlow — E-Learning Platform UI

Designing a focused learning experience for college students — structured courses, visible progress, and motivational loops that keep them engaged past day 3.

Problem Statement
Students dropped mid-course due to scattered content, invisible progress, and no sense of momentum — the platform felt like a content dump, not a learning journey.
My Role
UI/UX Designer — competitive analysis, user interviews, information architecture, responsive component design, and full Figma prototype.
Design Process
🔍 Research
→
📐 Wireframe
→
🎨 UI Design
→
⚡ Prototype
✅ Outcome
Built a cohesive learning dashboard with visual progress rings, a streak system, and a distraction-free reading mode. Navigation reduced from 5 levels to 2. Designed with WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility in mind throughout.
